Interview

Patty Valdovinos

Librarian III for Multilingual Collections

Interview Questions

1. Can you describe your role as a Librarian III for Multilingual Collections and how it fits within the broader mission of your library? How do you advocate internally for multilingual resources and services? How has the role evolved over time?

2. What technical, linguistic, or interpersonal skills are essential for success in your role?

Are there any skills or qualifications that you feel should be added to multilingual librarian job descriptions but aren’t currently required?

Which languages are most used in the library? Has this changed in your time as a librarian? Is there a gap between patron languages and librarians available?

What experiences before this role do you feel were formative in preparing you for this position?

3. How have you had to adapt as technologies or community demographics have changed?

Can you share an example of a time when a demographic shift directly changed your programming or collection strategy?

Have translation technologies (like AI tools) helped or complicated your work?

Are political changes and the current Trump administration impacting library services or patrons? How is the field responding to this?

4. What parts of your job are most visible to patrons or administrators?

What metrics or stories resonate most with library administrators when demonstrating the impact of the work you do?

How do you balance public-facing responsibilities with collection development and administrative work?

5. What kinds of maintenance or behind-the-scenes work are essential to keeping collections usable? What role do academic journals or ALA conferences play in your work? Are you able/is it helpful to consistently consult the research coming out of these places?

What role does consulting directly with community members play in your higher-level decision making and/or your day-to-day responsibilities?

6. How is AI impacting multilingual librarianship?

Do you think AI will impact the hiring qualifications for future librarians wanting to enter the field of multilingual librarianship?

7. Do you see AI as improving access, introducing new biases, or both?

8. What concerns or opportunities do you anticipate for the future of multilingual collections?

Do you feel this position is unique to LAPL or the LA community specifically, or is it a position that most libraries should strive to incorporate?
